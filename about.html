About Eco-Sync

Eco-Sync was created as a university assignment to demonstrate a small, multi-page website focused on environmental awareness. The goal is to present information clearly, work well on phones and desktops, and invite visitors to share feedback.

The site uses semantic HTML5, Bootstrap 5 for layout and components, and a custom stylesheet for a cohesive green and beige visual theme.
