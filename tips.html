Sustainability tips

Small changes add up. Try one new habit at a time and build from there.

  • Reduce single-use items — carry a water bottle, coffee cup, and reusable bag.
  • Save energy at home — switch off lights, use LED bulbs, and air-dry laundry when possible.
  • Eat more plants — even one or two meat-free meals per week lowers your footprint.
  • Repair before replacing — mend clothes, fix devices, or donate what you no longer need.
  • Travel smarter — walk, cycle, or use carpooling for short trips when you can.
